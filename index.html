About Me

Hi! I'm Arthi Bhoomireddy, an Applied Computer Science student at George Mason University with a focus in Business Management and a minor in Data Science. This site is a space to share the parts of my journey that don't really fit on my resume, but still make me who I am. I’m passionate about machine learning, data science, and the intersection of tech and impact. Whether I’m working on personal coding projects or exploring ways tech can improve daily life, I’m always striving to build skills that will one day help me contribute to something meaningful. My goal is to grow into a role where I can solve complex problems and deliver value, ideally at the intersection of product, data, and software. In my free time, you might find me reading, trying new dessert recipes, or watching a new anime. Feel free to take a look around. If anything here resonates with you, I’d love to connect!
